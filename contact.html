Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have outdoor seating?

Yes! Meatlovers features a beautiful outdoor patio perfect for Nairobi evenings. We have heaters available during cooler weather.

Do you take reservations?

Absolutely! You can book a table online through our website or call us. Walk-ins are always welcome too.

Do you have vegetarian options?

While we're a meat-focused restaurant, we offer several delicious vegetarian sides and salads. Ask your server for recommendations!

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have secured parking available for our guests at the back of the restaurant.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes, we offer takeaway services. Just call ahead or order at the counter.

Are you open on public holidays?

Yes, we're open on most public holidays from 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM. Check our Google Business Profile for holiday updates.
